Pick 10 whole numbers that sum to 1000 to contest stacks worth 1-10 gold. We will run one game for all pairs of submissions. Claim the most gold to win! Submissions close on Tuesday, Dec 10 at 11:00:00 PM EST.

The Setup

The year is 2050. You, general Blotto, have been called on to execute a daring strategy to secure critical resources that may decide the ongoing conflict. Only 1000 of your finest soldiers remain - do you have what it takes to secure victory?

How to Play

Overview

Blotto is a two-player game in which each player assigns nonnegative whole numbers ("soldiers") to 10 stacks numbered 1-10. Each player has 1000 soldiers. Your assignment is secret, so don't tell the other competitors!

The Goal

Each of the ten stacks has a certain amount of gold. Stack #1 has 1 gold, stack #2 has 2 gold, etc. Your task is to arrange your soldiers to claim the most gold.

How to Claim Gold

The player who assigns a higher number of soldiers to a stack wins that stack. In the case of a tie, neither player wins that stack, so you should avoid ties if you can!

Gameplay

Each player will decide on an assignment of their 1000 soldiers to stacks. For each pair of submissions, we'll run a round of Blotto. The winner is the person who claims the most total gold in all of the rounds.
